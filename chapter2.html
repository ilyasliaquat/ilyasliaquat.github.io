Noorani Qaida – Learn Quran Reading

Noorani Qaida is a beginner-friendly book that helps kids and adults learn to read the Quran in Arabic. It starts with the Arabic alphabet and step-by-step Tajweed rules to ensure proper pronunciation and fluency.!

Lesson 2: Understanding Murakkabat (Compound Letters)

In this lesson, we explore Murakkabat, or compound letters. When two or more letters are joined together, they form a Murakkab. This combination changes their individual forms, creating unique shapes that are essential to learn for proper reading.

Understanding Joint Letters (Murakkabat)

In this lesson, you will explore joint letters, also known as “Murakkabat.” When two or more letters are combined, they form a “Murakkab.” You will also learn how certain letters take different shapes when joined with others. By the end of this lesson, you will be able to recognize and understand the various forms of these letters, enhancing your reading and writing skills.
