The Tashdeed (Shaddah)

In this tutorial, students will learn the answers to the following important questions in the best possible way.

What is this Symbol ( ّ )?

This symbol is called Tashdeed or Shaddah.

What is the Name of the Tashdeed Letter?

A letter with Tashdeed is called Mushaddad.

How Many Times is the Letter with Tashdeed Read?

The letter with Tashdeed is read twice.

How is Mushaddad Read?

It is read with a strong voice.
