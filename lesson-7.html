Standing Fatha, Kasra, and Damma

Introduction

Prolonged Standing Fatha (نٰ), Standing Kasra (ھٖ or نٖ), and Standing Damma (اُ) are equal to one Alif.
Standing Fatha is equal to Alif Maddah.
Standing Kasra is equal to Ya Maddah.
Standing Damma is equal to Waw Maddah.
