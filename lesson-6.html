The Alphabets & Movement

Introduction

Learning how to connect letters and practicing their movement is essential. If students cannot connect letters correctly, they should revise the previous lesson. It is important to ensure they have fully mastered the previous lesson before moving forward. This is a fundamental rule, and students must achieve perfection in it.
