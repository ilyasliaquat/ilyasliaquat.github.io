The Exercise of Sukoon

When is Ikhfa Done on Noon Sakin and Tanween?

Ikhfa is applied when Noon Sakin (نْ) or Tanween (ً ٍ ٌ) is not followed by the Throatal Letters ( ء ه ع ح غ خ ).

When is “Raa” (ر) in Bold?

The letter ر is pronounced boldly when: The letter before it is not Ya Sakin (يْ). The letter before it does not have Kasra ( ِ ).

What are Bold Letters?

The following letters are pronounced in a bold (heavy) manner: ( ص – ض – ط – ظ – خ – غ – ق )

Symbols Indicating Where to Stop

There are four stopping symbols in the Quran: Waqf-e-Taam ( ۝ ) – Complete stop. Waqf-e-Lazim ( م ) – Mandatory stop. Waqf-e-Jaiz ( ج ) – Permissible stop. Waqf-e-Mutlaq ( ط ) – Preferred stop.
